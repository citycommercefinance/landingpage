This policy applies to personal data we process about: visitors to the Website; prospective and existing clients; individuals who contact us by phone, email, WhatsApp or our enquiry forms; and representatives of businesses and partners we deal with. It does not apply to third-party websites we link to (see Section 13).

## 4. The personal data we collect

Depending on how you interact with us, we may collect:

- **Identity & contact data** — name, job title, company, email address, telephone number and similar details you provide.
- **Enquiry & financial-interest data** — the financing requirement you tell us about (for example service of interest, indicative amount, sector, country, preferred date) submitted through our forms or in correspondence.
- **Know-Your-Customer (KYC) data** — where we proceed with a client relationship, identification and due-diligence information required by applicable anti-money-laundering (AML) and KYC obligations, such as identity documents, proof of address, company ownership details and source-of-funds information.
- **Communications data** — the content of your messages, emails and call notes.
- **Technical & usage data** — IP address, browser and device type, pages viewed and similar analytics data collected automatically via cookies and similar technologies (see Section 11).

We do not intentionally collect special-category or sensitive personal data through the Website. Please do not send us such data unless we specifically request it.

## 5. How we collect personal data

- **Directly from you** — when you complete an enquiry form, call or message us, or correspond with us.
- **Automatically** — through cookies and similar technologies when you use the Website.
- **From third parties** — for example banks, financial institutions, business partners, KYC/AML screening providers or publicly available sources, where relevant to a transaction.

## 6. Why we use your personal data, and our lawful basis

Under the PDPL we must have a lawful basis for each processing activity. The table below summarises ours:

| Purpose | Lawful basis (PDPL) |
|---|---|
| Respond to your enquiry and provide information you request | Your **consent**, and/or steps taken **at your request prior to a contract** |
| Assess and arrange financing facilities for you with banks/financial institutions | **Performance of a contract** / pre-contract steps at your request |
| Carry out KYC, AML and sanctions checks | **Compliance with a legal obligation** |
| Manage our client relationship and provide ongoing service | **Performance of a contract** / **legitimate business interest** |
| Improve and secure the Website (analytics, fraud/abuse prevention) | **Legitimate business interest**, and **consent** for non-essential cookies |
| Send you updates or marketing you have asked for | Your **consent** (you may withdraw it at any time) |

Where we rely on your consent, you may withdraw it at any time (see Section 12). Withdrawing consent does not affect processing already carried out.

## 7. Who we share your personal data with

We share personal data only where necessary, and with appropriate safeguards, including with:

- **Banks and financial institutions / funding partners** — to assess, structure and arrange the financing you ask us to facilitate.
- **Service providers** — IT, hosting, communications and professional advisers acting on our instructions.
- **Regulators, authorities and law-enforcement** — where required to comply with a legal or regulatory obligation (including AML/KYC).
- **Professional advisers** — lawyers, auditors and consultants, where relevant.

We do **not** sell your personal data. 

## 8. International / cross-border transfers

Some recipients may be located outside the UAE. Where we transfer personal data abroad, we do so only in accordance with the PDPL — that is, to a jurisdiction recognised as providing an adequate level of protection, or subject to appropriate safeguards, or with your explicit consent, or where another PDPL condition for transfer is met.

## 9. How long we keep your personal data

We keep personal data only for as long as necessary for the purposes set out above, including to meet legal, regulatory, tax, accounting and AML record-keeping requirements, after which it is securely deleted or anonymised. AML records retained for the period required by UAE law.

## 10. How we protect your personal data

We apply appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect personal data against loss, unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure or destruction, taking into account the nature and risks of the processing. ## 12. Your rights under the PDPL

Subject to the conditions and exceptions in the PDPL, you have the right to:

1. **Be informed** about how your personal data is processed;
2. **Access** your personal data and obtain a copy;
3. **Rectify** inaccurate or incomplete data;
4. **Erase** your data ("right to be forgotten") in the circumstances allowed by law;
5. **Restrict or stop** certain processing;
6. **Data portability** — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format and have it transferred where technically feasible;
7. **Object** to processing in the circumstances allowed by law;
8. **Withdraw consent** at any time where processing is based on consent;
9. **Lodge a complaint** with the UAE Data Office.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details in Section 2. We will respond within the timeframe required by the PDPL. We may need to verify your identity before acting on a request. Exercising these rights is free of charge in most cases.

If you are not satisfied with our response, you may complain to the **UAE Data Office** (the federal supervisory authority). +971 800 12 or email Info@tdra.gov.ae.
